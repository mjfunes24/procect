Education Required

Typically possess academic degree. The length of study is five years. The completed degree is designed as a bachelor in engineering. Task

The task of this career include design buildings & bridges. They also design space Stations, Airports and jet, Seaports, Energy Systems. They also need to have a good judgment of the estimate of the cost to build the certain project. Railroads, roads, and subways are design by these engineers too.

Jobs

Most of the Civil Engineers work a office buildings, Laboratories, and Industrial Plants. Others work in the open. When they work in office they only design that they are required. When they work in laboratories they test the materials that constructors use.

Salary

Relation with Computer Science
